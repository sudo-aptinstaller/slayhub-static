What can SlayHub Connector do?

SlayHub connector helps your customer facing teams of Zendesk or Freshdesk to connect with your product repositories of Azure DevOps or GitHub. This helps your avoid any enhance tracking the inflow of requests, issues or bugs thus helping you serve your customers better.

Create Work Items

With a simple to do action, create, update or link your customer tickets and update your product teams of priority actions. Set you custom labels, link it to right sprint teams.

Communicate

Send and sync comments between your customer facing and product teams. Decide what comments to be seen public vs private. Add attachments to understand the issues clear.

Secure data

SlayHub does not store your data, it helps bridge your Zendesk and FreshDesk platforms to DevOps or GitHub repositories. All the information is passed through Oauth authentication, removing any need for us to access your data.
